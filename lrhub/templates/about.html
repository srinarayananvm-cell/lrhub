About Us

Welcome to ArivuVerse — a universe of knowledge, collaboration, and growth.

This platform was proudly developed by Srinarayanan, a passionate Computer Science student from Madurai, with the guidance and support of Microsoft Copilot, an AI companion that helped architect and refine the project.

ArivuVerse is built to empower learners and educators by providing a space where learning resources, collaboration, and analytics come together seamlessly. It reflects a vision of making education accessible, engaging, and community‑driven.

Every feature — from collaboration tools to resource sharing and analytics — has been carefully designed to ensure clarity, usability, and empowerment for learners and educators alike.
